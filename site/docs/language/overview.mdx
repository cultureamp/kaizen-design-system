Use this guide to answer all your nitty gritty questions about how to write everything from lists to inspirations. It’s intended for use by Culture Amp employees as well as anyone who contributes content to our platform.

### **For Campers**

This guide is here to help you write to Culture Amp’s voice, tone and grammatical rules. Its purpose is to create consistency in the way we structure content across the platform. If you have any questions, give your friendly neighbourhood content strategists a shout.

### **For contributors**

We hope you find this guide useful. It should give you a good idea of what to do when you’re adapting your writing to Culture Amp’s style. If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us.
