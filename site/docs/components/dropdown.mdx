### When to use?

*   When you’re short on space and need to provide further links or actions.


### When not to use?

*   When you’re actually selecting a value from a list of options, use a [Select](https://drive.google.com/a/cultureamp.com/open?id=1DfHQktvjfgaY9nqNEHg1OxXbe-vpK1HuQ1Vz_P9v9E4) instead.
